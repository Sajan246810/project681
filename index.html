About Us

Mount Litera Zee School, Old Mahabalipuram Road (MLZS - Navalur), a world class school in Chennai that aims at setting a benchmark for progressive and holistic education in India. At MLZS South, learning becomes fun through various interactive sessions and activities. Our school follows CBSE curriculum that makes use of modern teaching methodologies achieved through smart classrooms and labs with in-built audio-visual systems.. Mount Litera Zee School is a noble endeavour by Zee Learn that has been in the domain of Indian education since 1994 to bring about a quantum improvement in school education Mount Litera Zee School, OMR Navalur endeavours to prepare leaders of the 21st century. It believes children are not just roll numbers; they are unique individuals, having infinite potential. Keeping the child in focus we filter all our decisions and actions. We ensure single- minded devotion to each child’s growth and development. Real understanding comes from an integrated approach, at MLZS we impart activity based learning to students, wherein they engage themselves and learn as per the saying "Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn." Our values at MLZS are more than just statements — they depict ways of life. We focus on Development of Self-Esteem Induction of Responsibility and Morality Harnessing Academic Potential and Academic Skills
